
Breakout Strategy Configuration Guide
====================================

1. Introduction
---------------
This document describes a breakout trading strategy optimized for the XAUUSDc symbol on the M5 timeframe.
It includes a detailed configuration of the strategy's parameters, theoretical foundations, and mathematical
formulas used to identify breakout events, confirm momentum, and manage risk.

-----------------------------------
2. Timeframe & General Settings
-----------------------------------
• **Timeframe**: 5-minute bars (M5)
• **Moving Averages**:
   - Fast: EMA(8)
   - Slow: EMA(21)
• **Capital**: $10,000
• **Risk per Trade**: 1% of account equity
• **Position Size**: Calculated dynamically using ATR and risk percentage

Formula for position size (lot size):
Lot Size = (Risk% × Capital) / (SL ATR × ATR × Contract Size)

---------------------------------
3. Trend Filter
---------------------------------
These parameters determine market conditions conducive to breakouts.

• **EMA 200**: Provides long-term trend direction
• **ADX (14)**: Measures strength of trend
  - Formula:
    ADX = EMA( | +DI - -DI | / (+DI + -DI) × 100 )
  - threshold > 25 considered a strong trend
• **Bollinger Band Width (BB Width)**:
  Ensures market is not in consolidation.
  - Formula:
    BB_width = (Upper_BB - Lower_BB) / Middle_BB

----------------------------------
4. Volatility Filter
----------------------------------
ATR-based filters avoid false signals during low volatility periods.

• **ATR Baseline Window**: 14
• **ATR Multiplier Minimum**: 1.1
• **ATR Multiplier Maximum**: 3.2
• **Spread ATR Max**: 0.08

ATR Formula:
ATR_t = EMA( True Range, N )
True Range = max(High_t - Low_t, |High_t - Close_{t-1}|, |Low_t - Close_{t-1}|)

Spread ATR Ratio = (Ask - Bid) / ATR

------------------------------------
5. Breakout Conditions
------------------------------------
Defines logic to identify valid breakouts.

• **Range Lookback**: 40 bars (n)
• **Range Min ATR**: 0.8 × ATR
• **Breakout Buffer**: 0.3 × ATR
• **Confirmation Bars**: 1

Logic:
- High_t > Max(High_{t-n:t-1}) + (Breakout Buffer × ATR)
- OR Low_t < Min(Low_{t-n:t-1}) - (Breakout Buffer × ATR)

------------------------------------
6. Momentum Confirmation
------------------------------------
Breakouts must be supported by momentum to avoid false signals.

• **Momentum Type**: Hybrid RSI + MACD
• **RSI Thresholds**:
   - Long: RSI_t > 60
   - Short: RSI_t < 40

RSI Formula:
RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS)), where RS = Avg Gain / Avg Loss

• **MACD Settings**: Fast = 12, Slow = 26, Signal = 9
• **MACD Histogram Threshold**: 0.0002

MACD Formula:
MACD = EMA(price, fast) - EMA(price, slow)
Histogram = MACD - Signal Line

Momentum Condition:
Enter only if (RSI and MACD both aligned in trend direction).

--------------------------------------
7. Trade Management
--------------------------------------
Defines how stop-loss and take-profit levels are set and adjusted.

• **SL ATR**: 1.5 × ATR
• **TP ATR**: 2.5 × ATR
• **Trailing Stop Trigger**: at 1.8 × ATR
• **Trailing Stop Multiplier**: 1.1

Trailing Stop Formula:
TSL = Entry ± (ATR × Trail Multiplier)

------------------------------------
8. Risk Guard
------------------------------------
Account protection rules:

• Max Daily Loss = 3% of Capital
• Max Consecutive Losses = 3
• Max Losses per Session = 2
• Cooldown Period = 60 minutes

----------------------------------
9. Summary
----------------------------------
This breakout strategy uses well-known technical tools such as ATR, RSI, MACD, and ADX to respond to changes in
quality of price action and volatility. It is optimized with respect to XAUUSD historical tick data.
